About Vadodara

Vadodara, also known as Baroda, is a city rich in cultural heritage and modern attractions. It is famous for the grand **Laxmi Vilas Palace**, beautiful parks, and vibrant festivals.

This city is an **artistic hub**, home to the prestigious Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda and numerous museums.
