About the Statue of Unity

The Statue of Unity located in Kevadia, Gujarat, is the tallest statue in the world, standing at 182 meters (597 feet). It is dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, India's Iron Man.

Unveiled on October 31, 2018, this engineering marvel represents India's unity and strength**. The site also includes a museum, viewing gallery, and scenic surroundings along the Narmada River.

Visitors can enjoy the laser light show, riverfront cycling, and adventure activities, making it a must-visit tourist destination in India.
